Answers to your
most common questions

What is Superteam?

Superteam is a co-operative of experienced creatives, developers and operators that helps Solana founders win and onboard talent into web3.

What does Superteam Balkan do?

The Balkans have many talents and success stories in the Solana ecosystem. Superteam will work on providing financial support and mentorship through knowledge and connections for the new generation of web3 entrepreneurs on their journey. This platform is intended as the first step towards enabling the success of both current and future young talents from the Balkan region.

How do I become a member?

You can become a member by being active in the community, helping with events, doing bounties and spreading the awareness about us.

What is the structure of our community?

Unlike many other communities, Superteam focuses on merit. There is no token you can buy to gain access. Instead, we require all participants in our community to engage through their work.

How do I start earning?

You can start earning by creating a profile on Superteam Earn and doing bounties.
